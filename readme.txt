Effortlessly start your very own online store on Wordpress with TackThis!


== Description ==

This simple-to-use plugin lets you build your own unique store, complete with an automated checkout system, inventory management, analytics and special promotional features. Start selling across the web, social media and mobile, Tack your shop on your Wordpress site and even on your Facebook page!

= Tack =
Effortlessly Tack your online store to any blog, websites and Facebook page, by embedding the code generated by TackThis onto your site. Your customers can shop wherever, whenever!

We have simplified the process for you. No HTML codes, CSS knowledge needed! Generate the your online store unique code at a click of a button. After building your store front, simply hit the "Tack Your Shop" button. You store's unique code would instantly be generated

= Sales Analytics =
Get monthly, daily and even hourly updates of your sales transactions and sales volume. TackThis feeds you the vital information needed to fine-tune your sales and marketing campaigns. 

= Inventory at a Glance =
Fret over stocks and invoicing no more! TackThis helps you keep a tab on your sales and the stock count, so that you can manage your inventory in a breeze.

= Customize your Shop Front =
TackThis lets you create a shop layout unique to you. Choose your colour palette and display layout at a click of a button. TackThis is made for those with absolutely no knowledge of coding! 

= Customer Service =
Providing excellent customer service is our forte. Our live chat service and friendly customer care agent will be there to guide you along the way. 

= Promotional Tools =
Offer special promotions to give your sales volume a boost. TackThis lets you to customize the percentage of discount and time frame for the promotion price. TackThis even allow you to create a shared promotion, which rewards customers for sharing your product on their Facebook wall! 

Add and manage your promotions easily with the Promotions tab. Create your promotions simply by selecting the date, set the discount value and choose the products your would like to apply the promotions to.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `tackthis.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Proceed to [TackThis](http://www.tackthis.com/shop/personal/pricing) to sign up for a plan of your choice.
4. Follow the instructions in the TackThis Dashboard to setup your shop.
